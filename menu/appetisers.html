Alergy Advice

Some of our dishes contain celery, cereals containing gluten, crustaceans, egg, fish, lupin, milk, molluscs, mustard, nuts, peanuts, sesame seeds, soya, sulphur dioxide which some people may have reaction to. Please inform our staff before ordering if you suffer from any food allergies. We will do our best to advise you.
